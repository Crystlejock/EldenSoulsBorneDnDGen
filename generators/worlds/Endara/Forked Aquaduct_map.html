Forked Aquaduct

Forked Aquaduct was once a bustling hub of trade and commerce. The aquaducts provided a steady flow of fresh water to the city, and the many shops and stalls were always doing brisk business. However, a cataclysmic event struck the city, and the aquaducts were destroyed. Now, the aquaducts are little more than a series of ruined bridges, spanning a deep chasm. The few remaining residents of the city are constantly tormented by the sound of eerie chants emanating from the chasm, and the threat of deadly traps set by the dark creatures that now inhabit the city.
