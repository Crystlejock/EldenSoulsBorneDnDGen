Festering Asylum of Cages

The Festering Asylum of Cages is a dark and dangerous place, home to madness and evil forces. It is said that the fog is so thick that it is difficult to see more than a few feet in front of you, and the ground is lined with webs. The constant chanting of an unknown voice sets a feeling of unease in all who hear it. Those who are unfortunate enough to find themselves in the Asylum quickly find themselves lost in the maze-like corridors. The walls are lined with cells, each containing a more demented and deranged inmate than the last. The atmosphere is one of despair and hopelessness, and it is said that the very air is thick with madness. The origins of the Asylum are shrouded in mystery, but it is said that it was built to contain those who were deemed too dangerous or too insane to be allowed to roam free. Over the years, it has become a place of dark legend, and few dare to venture
