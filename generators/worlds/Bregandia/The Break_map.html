The Break

The Break is a region of great cliffs and rocky terrain. It is a cold and desolate place, where only the hardiest of creatures can survive. Even the air is chilly and unforgiving. The people of The Break are known for their toughness and their love of life. They are a hardy people, who have to fight for everything they have. Even the animals of The Break are tough, and only the strongest survive. This is a land of survival of the fittest.
