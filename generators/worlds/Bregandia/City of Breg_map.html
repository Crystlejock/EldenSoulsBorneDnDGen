City of Breg

Breg is a city that was founded on the remains of an ancient kingdom. The kingdom was destroyed by a great war, but the people of the kingdom were able to rebuild it into a city. Breg is a thriving city that is known for its wealth and its abundance of life. The city is home to many farmers and small villages. The people of Breg are known for their hospitality and their love of life. Milo's Farmstead is a well-known fixture of Breg that is known for its fresh produce and its hospitable atmosphere.
