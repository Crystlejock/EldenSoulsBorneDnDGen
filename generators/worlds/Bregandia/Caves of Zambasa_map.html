Caves of Zambasa

The Caves of Zambasa are an infamous labyrinth of tunnels and caverns that honeycomb the entirety of the Sands. It is said that within these caves, there is a lost city of great wealth and treasure. Many have ventured into the Caves of Zambasa in search of this fabled city, but none have ever returned. The Caves of Zambasa are also home to a more deadly inhabitant: the sandworms. These massive creatures burrow through the sand, lying in wait to devour anything that crosses their path. The Caves of Zambasa are a treacherous place, and only the fittest will survive.
