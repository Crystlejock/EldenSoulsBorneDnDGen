The Citadel

The Citadel is an ancient stronghold located in the Flats region. It is said to have been built by a powerful wizard who used it as a base of operations for his research into the magical arts. The Citadel is now a ruin, but it is still steeped in magic. The wizard's library and laboratories still remain, and many of his books and scrolls can still be found there. The Citadels is also home to a number of powerful magical creatures, including a trio of Dracoliches. The Citadel is a popular destination for adventurers, as it is said that anyone who can find the wizard's lost treasure will be granted immense power.
