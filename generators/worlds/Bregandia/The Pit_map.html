The Pit

The Pit is a vast and treacherous desert where only the fittest survive. It is a land of extremes, where the sun beats down mercilessly during the day and the cold creeps in at night. Travelers in The Sands must be constantly on the lookout for signs of life, as the desert is home to many deadly creatures. The most dangerous of these are the sandworms, gigantic creatures that burrow through the sand, waiting to devour anything that crosses their path. Those who brave the desert often do so in search of ancient treasures, lost cities, or other riches. Others simply seek to find a way out. Either way, The Sands is a dangerous place, and one that should not be taken lightly.
