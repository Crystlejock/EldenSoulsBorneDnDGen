The Karath Monestary

The Karath Monastery is a secluded monastery located in the middle of The Flats region. The monastery is a beautiful and peaceful place, with a strong atmosphere of faith and life. The monastery is home to many monks who live a simple and humble life. The monastery is also home to a number of ancient artifacts and texts, which are said to hold great power and wisdom. The monastery is a popular destination for adventurers and pilgrims alike, who come seeking the knowledge and power that is said to be held within its walls.
